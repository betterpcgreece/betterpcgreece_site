Principles

What betterPC stands for

01
Honest pricing

Prices are estimates, labelled as such. No inflated numbers to make builds look cheap by comparison.

02
No ads, no affiliates

betterPC earns nothing from recommending specific brands. Picks are based on value and availability.

03
Old hardware matters

DDR2 and DDR3 machines can still serve real purposes. Helping people get more life from them is worth it.

04
Compatibility first

Nothing is recommended without a check. CPU–mobo–RAM compatibility is flagged before you waste money.

05
Greek context

Prices, availability, and part advice are grounded in the Greek and broader EU market, not the US.

06
Always improving

Parts lists, prices, and guides are updated as the market changes. This isn't a set-and-forget site.
